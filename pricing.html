How pricing works

Transparent enough to trust. Flexible enough to be fair.

A fixed price can be unfair when two hotels have completely different systems, staff habits and operational complexity. That is why Vigil shows starting prices, then confirms the final service package after implementation review.

What affects the price

  • ✓ Number of rooms, villas or units
  • ✓ Departments included: reception, housekeeping, maintenance, security, F&B or finance
  • ✓ Communication channels: WhatsApp, email, website chat, Slack or others
  • ✓ Existing systems and integrations
  • ✓ Reporting depth for owners and managers
  • ✓ Training and monthly support level
