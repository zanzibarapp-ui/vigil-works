Main features

The hotel, simplified.

Vigil is not another complicated dashboard. It is a calm operational assistant that notices, reminds, assigns, follows up and reports.

💬

Guest messages

Answers FAQs, prepares replies, detects unhappy guests, and escalates sensitive messages to management.

🧹

Staff tasks

Creates housekeeping, maintenance, security and front-office tasks with clear instructions in the right language.

📅

Bookings

Reads reservations from OTA, PMS or channel manager and turns them into arrivals, room prep and guest communication.

🔧

Maintenance

Tracks problems by room, asset and priority, so AC, water, internet, generator and room issues do not disappear.

⭐

Reputation

Flags complaint patterns before checkout and helps prepare review replies for Booking.com, Airbnb and Google.

📊

Owner briefings

Sends simple daily summaries: arrivals, departures, incidents, staff follow-ups, revenue notes and tomorrow’s risks.

Guest journey

From first message to final review.

Vigil follows the guest experience in stages, so the team knows what should happen next.

Before arrivalWelcome message, arrival time, airport transfer, dietary needs, special requests.
Check-inRoom readiness, guest registration, balance payment, welcome script, luggage and orientation.
During stayHousekeeping preferences, restaurant bookings, tours, complaints, maintenance and upsells.
CheckoutBill check, transfer reminder, room inspection, lost property and review request.
After stayReview reply, repeat-booking offer and guest-history notes for future visits.

Safety & control

AI with boundaries.

The owner decides what Vigil can do automatically and what needs human approval.

Approval rules

  • ✓ Draft guest replies, manager approves
  • ✓ Auto-answer only safe FAQs
  • ✓ Escalate complaints, refunds and sensitive issues
  • ✓ Never change prices or approve refunds without permission
  • ✓ Track staff tasks without replacing management judgement
Failover plan

When something goes offline.

Hotels have power cuts, weak WiFi and busy staff. The system must have a manual backup.

Fallbacks

  • ✓ Backup WhatsApp group for urgent operations
  • ✓ Manual housekeeping checklist if Slack is unavailable
  • ✓ Integration health check every morning
  • ✓ Clear escalation tree for security, water, power and internet
  • ✓ Owner alert if bookings, cameras or messages disconnect
Data privacy

Only the access needed.

Because hotel systems contain guest data, staff details, payments and camera access, you deside how access is handled.

Data safety checklist

  • ✓ Define who can see owner, staff, guest and finance reports
  • ✓ Use separate accounts where possible, not personal passwords
  • ✓ Store only the data required to operate the system
  • ✓ Document how access can be revoked
  • ✓ Set camera and staff-monitoring boundaries clearly
