The concept

What is a Digital Twin?

Zuri doesn't run on a generic hotel template. It learns Beach Boheme specifically — your rooms, your people, your standards. The more complete the data, the smarter it runs from day one.

01 — Property

Your property, mapped exactly

Every villa, room, shared space, and facility. Every maintenance item. Every standard operating procedure. Zuri learns Beach Boheme's physical reality so it assigns the right task to the right person at the right time.

02 — Staff

Your team, known by name

Zuri knows each team member — role, language, shift, performance history, communication style. It messages your housekeeper in Swahili with step-by-step instructions. It briefs security at 18:00 every night without being asked.

03 — Systems

Every platform, connected

Booking.com, Airbnb, your PMS, CCTV cameras, router. Zuri reads incoming reservations automatically, monitors security, and integrates with every tool you already use — no double-entry, no manual updates.

04 — Standards

Your rules, enforced always

What does a perfect room look like at Beach Boheme? What happens if Kelvin doesn't check in? Your standards become Zuri's operating rules — enforced 24 hours a day, 365 days a year.

24/7 Operations

What Zuri does while you sleep

Zuri runs 16 scheduled jobs every day — from the 06:00 morning briefing to the 22:00 night summary. No instructions required.

🌅
Morning Briefing
At 06:00 every morning, Zuri reads overnight events, checks arrivals, and sends the owner a concise brief — occupancy, urgent tasks, staff performance, and today's focus. You wake up already informed.
🧹
Task Dispatch
Zuri creates and assigns tasks to specific staff — in their language, with exact instructions. Housekeeping knows each room's requirements. Maintenance gets photo-verified tickets. Late tasks trigger automatic chases.
📷
CCTV Monitoring
Zuri's vision agent scans camera feeds for security events — unusual movement, unattended areas, gate breaches. Alerts fire to the owner channel in real-time. Kelvin's night check-ins are tracked automatically.
📅
Reservation Intelligence
New booking arrives from Booking.com or Airbnb — Zuri creates the guest profile, assigns arrival tasks, briefs the kitchen on dietary needs, and sends a personalised welcome message. All within seconds.
📊
Performance Coaching
Every staff member gets a performance score that updates in real-time. Zuri's HR agent sends daily coaching lessons to those who need improvement. The weekly report names names and shows trends.
🌙
Night Summary
At 22:00, Zuri writes the full day report — revenue, incidents resolved, staff highlights, tomorrow's arrivals. Sergei reads a complete picture of Beach Boheme in 60 seconds, from anywhere in the world.

For Zuri to monitor security cameras and integrate with your network, we need physical access to your router and camera system during installation. Please have this information ready.

WiFi Router
We need access to your router admin panel
We configure network ports for Zuri's server, set static IPs for cameras, and verify connectivity across all property zones.
Router brand and model
e.g. TP-Link Archer C6, ASUS RT-AX88U
Router admin URL
Usually 192.168.0.1 or 192.168.1.1 — check back of router
Admin password
Printed on router sticker, or "admin" if never changed
WiFi coverage areas

Where each camera points: entrance, pool, reception, corridor, etc.
